🏆 The 5 Pillars of Success

These five principles will determine your success with this protocol. Master these, and you will achieve complete elimination.

1Elimination is Everything

You MUST have 2-3 bowel movements daily. Toxins are leaving through your colon. If they stay trapped, they recirculate and cause symptoms.

Action Steps:

  • Increase binders if elimination is slow
  • Drink more water (12+ glasses daily)
  • Add magnesium citrate (400mg at night) if needed
  • Consider enemas during parasite phase (2-3x per week)
  • Take vitamin C to bowel tolerance if constipated

2Binders Are Non-Negotiable

Binders capture toxins as they're released and escort them out. Without binders, toxins recirculate through enterohepatic circulation, causing worse symptoms than if you hadn't started the cleanse.

Action Steps:

  • Take binders 2 hours away from food and supplements (they bind everything)
  • Increase binder dosage during die-off symptoms
  • Always drink extra water with binders (16-20oz)
  • Don't skip binders even if feeling good—toxins are still releasing
  • Keep extra activated charcoal on hand for emergencies

3Liver Support is the Foundation

Your liver processes every single toxin, pathogen, and metabolic waste product. During this protocol, it's working overtime. Support it aggressively or risk overwhelming your detox capacity.

Action Steps:

  • 3 liver juices daily—no exceptions (minimum 1 if time-constrained)
  • Take liver supplements twice daily throughout all 28 days
  • Castor oil packs over liver 3-4x per week
  • Avoid alcohol, processed foods, and excess caffeine
  • Support bile flow with bitter foods (arugula, dandelion, artichoke)

4Die-Off is Normal—Support It

Feeling worse temporarily means it's working. When parasites, fungi, and pathogens die, they release toxins. This is called a Herxheimer reaction or "die-off." It's temporary and manageable.

Action Steps:

  • Expect Days 10-14 to be the most challenging
  • Increase binders during die-off (take 3-4x daily if needed)
  • Switch to baking soda morning elixir for alkalizing support
  • Take daily Epsom salt + baking soda baths (pulls toxins through skin)
  • Rest more, reduce exercise intensity temporarily
  • Stay hydrated (12-15 glasses water during peak die-off)
  • Don't quit—die-off means you're winning

5Sequence Matters—Trust the Process

This protocol is sequenced for maximum effectiveness and safety. Liver first, fungus second, parasites third, metals last. Don't skip phases or change the order.

Action Steps:

  • Complete the full 7 days of fungal pre-conditioning before adding parasite formula
  • Don't start heavy metal chelation before Day 22
  • Continue liver support throughout all phases
  • Follow supplement dosage increases as directed
  • Trust that each phase prepares you for the next

  • Add magnesium citrate 400-600mg at night

Severe Die-Off Symptoms

Symptoms May Include:

Extreme fatigue, severe headache, body aches, nausea, dizziness, irritability, brain fog

Emergency Protocol:
  1. Stop all anti-parasitic/antifungal supplements for 24 hours
  2. Drink baking soda water (½ tsp in 8oz water) immediately
  3. Take activated charcoal (4 capsules) with 20oz water
  4. Take immediate Epsom salt bath (2 cups salt, 1 cup baking soda, 30-45 min)
  5. Rest completely—sleep if possible
  6. Drink 20+ glasses water over next 24 hours
  7. Consider enema to flush toxins
  8. Resume protocol at 50% dosage the next day, then gradually increase

Nausea

Solutions:
  • Take all supplements with food (never on empty stomach)
  • Reduce parasite formula by 50% temporarily
  • Increase ginger intake (fresh ginger tea, ginger juice, crystallized ginger)
  • Switch to smaller, more frequent meals (5-6 per day)
  • Drink peppermint tea between meals
  • Temporarily reduce fat intake if severe
  • Take digestive enzymes with every meal

Insomnia / Sleep Disruption

Solutions:
  • Take magnesium glycinate (400-600mg) 1 hour before bed
  • Do Epsom salt bath before bed (magnesium absorbs through skin)
  • Avoid stimulating supplements after 3 PM (oregano oil, some antiparasitics)
